## Content Types & Formats

### Threads
- 5-10 tweets
- Hook in first tweet
- Clear narrative arc
- CTA in final tweet

### Single Posts
- Under 280 characters
- One clear message
- Visual when possible

### Blog Posts
- 800-1500 words
- SEO-optimized
- Clear sections
- CTA at end

### Videos/GIFs
- Under 30 seconds
- No audio required
- Clear product demonstration
- Looping friendly
